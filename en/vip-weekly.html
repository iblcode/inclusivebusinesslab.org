VIP Weekly

VIP Weekly are cycles of lessons for high school students on Values – Improvisation – Presentation.

Each VIP cycle starts with a "V-class", where the central topic is a value such as love, forgiveness or positive attitude. Students are then encouraged to write stories from their own experience based on the value discussed in class. The "I-class" is designed to improve communication and building confidence through situation-based improvisation. The cycle ends with a "P-class", where students present and discuss their stories from the V-class.
