Global Inclusive Leadership Challenges

The Challenges are a series of events, in which global leaders and experts from the different disciplines will share their stories of leadership journeys, present exciting discoveries in their own fields and give the next generation challenging questions on the real-world issues.

MOTIVATION

The Challenges aim to broaden the horizons of the next generation, inform them about global problems and encourage them to think deeply about the issues involved, helping them to become creative and ethically-minded problem solvers. The next generation can also gain insight into what potential employers are seeking in tomorrow’s leaders. The significant lives of these exemplary individuals can inspire the next generation to become inclusive leaders themselves.
