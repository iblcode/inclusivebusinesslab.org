STEM+ Studio is a suite of leadership development initiatives, where we promote 

strength in the STEM subjects and their contribution to wider society.

The STEM subjects (Science, Technology, Engineering and Mathematics) play an important role in shaping social development and securing economic growth by driving innovation in business, industry, arts and culture. As part of our global leadership program, we promote strength in STEM and high-level thinking skills to support the leaders of the future, including those from less affluent regions, in stepping onto the global stage. These STEM+ leadership initiatives, collectively called STEM+ Studio, are providing opportunities for next generation to grapple with a wide range of STEM-based challenges and see how these topics relate to the real-world applications such as:

  • Art & Culture
  • Communication & Media
  • Environment & Health
  • Sports
  • Communities & Societies
  • Entrepreneurship & Business

MathCafé Challenges

The MathCafé Challenges are creative tasks requiring students to work together to propose, discuss and develop their own innovative and original ideas. By participating in a Challenge, students gain much more than STEM skills; they also develop skills in teamwork and leadership, art and design, problem solving, speaking English, communication and presentation.

Espresso

Espresso is a group of activities run in conjunction with MathCafé. Whereas MathCafé is aimed at students of all backgrounds and abilities, Espresso activities are targeted the brightest students.

STEM + Leadership Weeks

STEM + Leadership Weeks consist of leadership development workshops and group activities designed to train next generation about STEM and its real-world applications and contribution to the society. An example topic is the use of nanotechnology in cancer treatment.
